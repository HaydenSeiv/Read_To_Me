"Are you sure this shortcut leads back to town?” Maya, a young woman with long brown hair and curious eyes, asked as she brushed branches out of her way. “Of course it does,” Leo, a tall boy with messy blond hair and a grin that tried too hard, replied with forced confidence, though his eyes darted nervously between the trees. “You said that an hour ago,” Maya muttered, stopping to tie her shoe. Leo sighed, then grinned sheepishly. “Okay, maybe I’m lost—but at least we’re lost together."
